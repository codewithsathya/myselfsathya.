About us

To the young and inquisitive minds out there, with its tagline 'Knowledge Runs Free' and with its foundation on the pillars of Innovation, Information and Intelligence, Wissenaire the Annual Techno-Management Fest of IIT Bhubaneswar brings to you Sanchaar - The Out-House Workshop Program, in association with some of the prominent names in the tech industry, an extraordinary opportunity to hone your skills and expertise in your field of interest along with valuable hands on experience.
